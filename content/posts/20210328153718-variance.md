## DEFINATION {#defination}

方差是在概率论和统计方差衡量随机变量或一组数据时离散程度的度量。


### Formula {#formula}

\begin{equation}
\\[S^2 = \frac{\sum (x\_i - \bar{x})^2}{n - 1}\\]
\\[S^2 = {sample\ variance}\\]
\\[x\_i = {the\ value\ of\ the\ one\ observation}\\]
\\[\bar{x} = the\ mean\ value\ of\ all\ observations\\]
\\[n = the number of observations\\]
\end{equation}
