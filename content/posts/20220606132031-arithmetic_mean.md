## Definition {#definition}

是一组数据之和，除以这组数据个数/项数。


### Denoted {#denoted}

\\[ {\bar {x}} \\] (read x bar)


## Formula {#formula}

\\[ {\bar {x}}={\frac {\sum \_{{i=1}}^{n}x\_{i}}{n}}={\frac {x\_{1}+x\_{2}+\cdots +x\_{n}}{n}} \\]
