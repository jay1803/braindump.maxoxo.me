## Definition {#definition}

用来衡量每单位资产创造多少净利润的指标。
Higher ROA indicates more asset efficiency.

资产收益率 = [Net Profit]({{< relref "20210822024724-net_profit.md" >}}) / [Total Assets]({{< relref "20210413133347-total_assets.md" >}})
